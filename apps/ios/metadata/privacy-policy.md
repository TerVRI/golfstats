# RoundCaddy Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 16, 2026**

## Introduction

RoundCaddy ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
- **Apple ID**: When you sign in with Apple, we receive your Apple ID email (which may be hidden if you choose) and name
- **Profile Data**: Any profile information you choose to add

### Golf Data
- **Rounds**: Scores, statistics, and notes from your golf rounds
- **Shots**: GPS coordinates of shots you mark
- **Statistics**: Calculated metrics like handicap, fairways hit, and strokes gained

### Location Data
- **GPS Coordinates**: Used to calculate distances to greens and track shots
- **Course Location**: To show weather and nearby courses
- Location data is only collected when the app is in use (or in background during an active round with your permission)

### Device Information
- Device type and operating system version
- App version
- Crash reports and performance data (anonymized)

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide core app functionality (GPS tracking, scoring, statistics)
- Sync data between your iPhone and Apple Watch
- Calculate your handicap and strokes gained metrics
- Save and retrieve your round history
- Improve app performance and fix bugs

## Data Storage

- Your data is stored securely on Supabase cloud servers
- Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest
- We retain your data as long as your account is active
- You can request data deletion at any time

## Data Sharing

**We do not sell your personal information.**

We may share data with:
- **Service Providers**: Supabase (database), Open-Meteo (weather data)
- **Legal Requirements**: If required by law or to protect rights

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: Request a copy of your data
- **Correct**: Update inaccurate information
- **Delete**: Request deletion of your account and data
- **Export**: Download your golf data

To exercise these rights, contact us at [your-email@example.com].

## Location Privacy

- Location is only used to calculate golf distances and track shots
- You can disable location access in iOS Settings at any time
- Disabling location will limit GPS tracking features
- We do not share precise location data with third parties

## Apple Watch

- Watch app communicates only with paired iPhone via WatchConnectivity
- Watch data syncs to your account through the iPhone app
- Same privacy protections apply to Watch data

## Children's Privacy

RoundCaddy is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
- Secure authentication via Sign in with Apple
- Regular security audits

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or email.
