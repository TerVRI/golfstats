## Description (4000 chars max)

RoundCaddy is your ultimate golf companion, combining GPS distance tracking, digital scorekeeping, and advanced statistics in one beautiful app – now with Apple Watch support.

**GPS DISTANCE TRACKING**
• Real-time distances to front, center, and back of every green
• Shot tracking with automatic distance calculations
• Works offline once course data is loaded

**DIGITAL SCORECARD**
• Quick score entry for each hole
• Track fairways hit, greens in regulation, and putts
• Automatic handicap calculation
• Round history with detailed statistics

**APPLE WATCH INTEGRATION**
• Glanceable distances on your wrist
• Enter scores directly from your watch
• Mark shots with GPS coordinates
• Seamless sync with iPhone

**COURSE DATABASE**
• Access to famous courses worldwide
• Live weather conditions at each course
• Add custom courses and tee boxes

**STROKES GAINED ANALYTICS**
• Professional-level statistics
• Identify strengths and weaknesses
• Track improvement over time

**DESIGNED FOR GOLFERS**
• Clean, easy-to-read interface
• Works in bright sunlight
• Quick actions for common tasks
• No subscription required for core features

Whether you're a weekend warrior or serious competitor, RoundCaddy helps you play smarter and lower your scores.

Download RoundCaddy today and take your game to the next level!
